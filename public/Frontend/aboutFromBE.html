The Generics

The Generics is an English rock band, formed in Chennai in 2016, that comprises Vegeta, Picolo, Krillin, Yamcha and Tien. Today, the Generics is regarded as the most influential band of all time and has become integral to the development of 21st century's counterculture and popular music's recognition as an art form. Rooted in skiffle, beat and 1950s rock 'n' roll, the Generics sound incorporats the elements of classical music and traditional pop in innovative ways; the band also explored music styles ranging from folk and Indian music to psychedelia and hard rock. As pioneers in recording, songwriting and artistic presentation, the Generics has revolutionised many aspects of the music industry and is often publicised as leaders of the era's youth and contemporary sociocultural movements.
